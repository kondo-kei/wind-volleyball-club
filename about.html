First Season

What to Expect

Wind Volleyball Club is building something new — and we want to build it the right way. Our first season will be shaped by the athletes and families who join us.

The schedule, tournament participation, and practice cadence will be determined based on the demand and commitment level of our initial player group. This means you have a real voice in how this club takes shape.

What won't change: the quality of coaching, the intensity of training, and our commitment to developing every athlete who walks through the door.

What We Know

Age Groups: 13U and 14U confirmed, with a potential high school team based on interest
Training Location: The Prairie School
Coaching: Kei Kondo and Henry Yunker leading all programs
Schedule: Practice and tournament schedule to be finalized based on player commitment
Philosophy: Competitive, developmental, and athlete-centered
