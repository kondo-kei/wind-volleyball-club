Year One

The First Season

Here's what our inaugural season looks like — from tryouts through the end-of-season tournament.

September 2025

Tryouts & Roster Set

Open tryouts for all age-eligible players. Final roster announced within 72 hours.

October 2025

Pre-Season Training Block

Six weeks of fundamentals, systems, and team-building before competition begins.

November 2025

Regular Season Begins

League play kicks off. Home matches at The Prairie School.

January 2026

Mid-Season Invitational

Two-day tournament with clubs from the region — our first big test.

March 2026

End-of-Season Tournament

Season culminates with the regional championship. Let's make a statement.
