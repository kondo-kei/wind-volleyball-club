Who We Are

About the Club

Wind Volleyball Club strips away the noise — experienced coaches, more playing time, and none of the travel, cost, or pressure that club volleyball doesn't need.

Our Philosophy

At Wind Volleyball Club, we believe the best way to develop players is simple — give them plenty of time with a ball in their hands, guided by experienced coaches who know the game. We keep our program local and intentional: overnight travel and far-away tournaments aren't something we pursue unless a team has genuinely earned a shot at national-level competition unavailable in our region. We proudly support multi-sport athletes and the families working hard to make that balance a reality — we ask for commitment to the program and, more importantly, to your teammates, but we have no interest in making volleyball an unreasonable burden and will always work with you to find a path that works.
