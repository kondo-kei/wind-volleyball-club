Badger Region Membership

Why we require it, and how to get yours.

Why We Require Membership

Wind Volleyball Club is a member of Badger Region Volleyball Association, a regional branch of USA Volleyball. Our insurance coverage is provided through this affiliation.

Because of this, all participants — including those attending tryouts, camps, and clinics — are required to hold an active Badger Region / USA Volleyball membership. This protects players in the event of unforeseen injuries or accidents.

Played Club in 2025–26? You're Likely Covered

If your player participated on a club volleyball team during the 2025–26 season, their membership most likely covers summer activities — including our tryouts and clinics.

You can find your Badger Region / USA Volleyball Membership ID by logging into your SportsEngine account. Look under your player's profile for a membership or registration tied to Badger Region Volleyball.

New to Club Volleyball? Purchase a Membership

If your player did not participate in club volleyball during the 2025–26 season, you'll need to purchase a Badger Region Volleyball membership before attending any Wind VC events.

A summer membership is $20 and covers all summer activities including tryouts, camps, and clinics.
